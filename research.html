I am interested in questions around understanding imperfection in valued fields (possibly with extra structure), both in the literal sense of imperfect valued fields, and more philosophically in relation to valued difference fields when the endomorphism is not surjective. More specifically,
  • I am interested in relative quantifier elimination (and other transfer principles) for valued difference field, especially in the non-inversive case,
  • In a joint project with Margarete Ketelsen and Piotr Szewczyk, I am interested in understanding definable henselian valuations and their relationship with defect.
