Research Interests

I am broadly interested in the model theory of valued fields and its applications.
  • My main interest is understanding the model theory of valued difference fields, especially when the endomorphism is not surjective. I am particularly curious about the parallels between failure of surjectivity and imperfection, and how machinery from the model theory of valued fields (e.g. defect) can be adapted to this setting.
  • I am also interested in the problem of finding definable henselian valuations on henselian fields. For example, I am interested in characterizing which fields admit one. This is a joint project with Margarete Ketelsen and Piotr Szewczyk.
