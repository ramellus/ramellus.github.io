🍪 I like thinking about the algebraic and arithmetic model theory of valued fields. I am interested in axiomatizing valued fields with extra structure, like isometries or contracting endomorphisms, and in the definability of henselian valuations.

🧭 My research is supported by the Deutsche Forschungsgemeinschaft (DFG, German Research Foundation) under the project Modelltheorie bewerteter Körper mit Endomorphismus, and via the Excellence Strategy EXC 2044–390685587, Mathematics Münster: Dynamics–Geometry–Structure.
