Podcast

Together with the science communication team of Mathematics Münster, I am producing a podcast called On A Tangent, of which I am also the host. In each episode I meet a different early career mathematician connected to the Cluster of Excellence, and explore their journey towards mathematics and their hopes for the future. You can find the podcast on the website of Mathematics Münster, on Spotify, and as an RSS feed on Podigee.
