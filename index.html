Research Interests

I am broadly interested in the model theory of valued fields and its applications. More specifically,
  • Valued difference fields: my main interest is understanding the model theory of valued fields endowed with non-surjective endomorphisms. I am particularly curious about the parallels between non-surjectivity and imperfection, and how machinery from the model theory of valued fields (e.g. defect) can be adapted to this setting.
  • Definable henselian valuations: I am also interested in the problem of finding definable henselian valuations. For example, I am interested in finding a characterization of which fields admit one. This is a joint project with Margarete Ketelsen and Piotr Szewczyk.
