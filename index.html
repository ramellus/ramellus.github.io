Simone Ramello
We’re all stories in the end.

I am a student of the Master in Comunicazione della Scienza “Franco Prattico”, in Trieste, Italy. Before that, I did a PhD in mathematics in Münster, Germany; even before that, I got my BSc and MSc in mathematics from the University of Torino, Italy. My grandma keeps asking when I'll stop moving around.
